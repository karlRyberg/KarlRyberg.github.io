The risk of an explosion is low, but to prevent explosions altogether, the ATEX directives need to be followed.

The directives contain information on precautions that need to be taken when working with explosive substances such as CNG. In addition to this, the premises used to house a CNG truck need to be designed bearing an explosion risk in mind. But since you already have CNG trucks in your fleet, this is probably no news to you!
