Background

Over the world, different measures are being taken to stabilise the global warming effect and meet the target of a maximum temperature increase of 2.0 â—‹C set by the Paris Agreement. To meet this, we need to dramatically reduce CO2 emissions related to human activity. This concerns all sectors, from energy to residential and transportation. In Europe, road commercial vehicles account for 5 % of the total CO2 emissions. Obviously, road transport is not the most important emission contributor. But as we are part of the problem, we need to be part of the solution!

As an example of what is done in the heavy-duty transport sector, a European regulation for CO2 emissions is now being prepared. It will require truck manufacturers to declare individual CO2 emissions for their trucks, starting from 1st of January 2019. The aim with this is to reduce CO2 emissions by making sure that only the most CO2-efficient solutions are brought to the market.
