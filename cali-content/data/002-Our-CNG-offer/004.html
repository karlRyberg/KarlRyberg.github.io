Customer segments and types

Reciclaje del Sur provides waste management service to the city of Madrid. This customer segment was what we initially focused on with our D Wide CNG offer. We have since broadened our Wide CNG offer to target distribution applications as well, such as parcel and post, consumer goods and daily commodities.

Customer segments and types

PARCEL AND POST

Solid mixed goods of different shape; smaller units brought together. Often palletized goods and goods consolidated at terminals for several owners.

Customer segments and types

CONSUMER GOODS

Goods bought and used by consumers except daily consumables. Transported from factories and warehouses to shops or final customer.

Customer segments and types

WASTE AND RECYCLING

Collection of waste from companies or homes to point of disposal, treatment or recycling. Operated by either municipalities or private companies.

Customer segments and types

DAILY COMMODITIES

Daily consumables for shops, restaurants and final consumers. Examples are food, pharmaceuticals and household consumables. Often climate controlled and with high hygienic requirements.
